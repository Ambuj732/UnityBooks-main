Return & Cancellation

Unity Books Offers both Cancellation and Return of Orders

  1. Cancellation Policy

    Cancellation fee will be charged on account of the cancellation of orders by the customer. A cancellation fee shall be charged based on the time the customer opts to cancel upon placing the order. There will be free cancellation for the orders only for the initial few hours of placing an order(Before the Order is Processed/Packed). After this time frame, a Cancellation Fee will be charged.

  2. Why is the Cancellation Fee charged?

    A cancellation fee is charged to compensate for the time and effort incurred in processing an order and the logistics service to ship the order.

  3. How will the Cancellation Fee be charged?

    The Cancellation Fee will be deducted from the amount paid by the customer for the cancelled order. The Refund will be credited back through the original payment method. If the customer has opted for Cash on Delivery/Pay on Delivery, the Cancellation Fee would be deducted from the Next order.

  4. Unity Books reserves the right to modify/waive off the Cancellation fee from time to time. You shall be responsible for making payments to Unity Books on account of any cancellation of orders from your end.

  5. Return Policy

    Returns are only acceptable if the return request is initiated within the return window period(24 Hours from the delivery of the product).

  6. Return will be processed if and only:

    it is determined that the product was not damaged while in your possession, the product is not different from what was shipped to you, the product is returned in original condition (with all the accessories).

  7. Return Options: Refund or Replacement . Products may be eligible for replacement only if the exact same item is in stock. incase if there is no stock available, then a refund would be issued.

  8. Refund will be received in the Unity Wallet as Wallet cash(UnityCoins) . Customers can use the Wallet cash anytime to make Orders through Unity Books. See the UnityCoins & Wallet section to know more about Unity Wallet.

  9. Note that the Refund process might take 3-5 Business Days in both the cases.

Cookie Policy

  1. What are Cookies?

    A cookie is a piece of software code that an internet website sends to your browser when you access information at that site. A cookie is stored as a simple text file on your computer or mobile device by a website’s server and only that server will be able to retrieve or read the contents of that cookie. Cookies let you navigate between pages efficiently as they store your preferences.
    Generally speaking, cookies do not contain personal information from which you can be identified, unless you have furnished such information to the Website.

  2. Why we Use Cookies?

    Like most interactive websites, we uses cookies to retrieve and recognize user details for each visit. Cookies are used in some areas of our site to help the functionality of this area and ease of use for those people visiting. It also helps us to understand which parts of the Website are most popular, and generally to assess user behaviour and characteristics in order to measure interest in and use of the various areas of the Website.

    • Keeping track of your preferences.
    • Keeping track of items stored in your shopping cart.

  4. How can i set Cookie Settings?

    You can manage browser cookies through your browser settings.All modern browsers allow you to change your cookie settings. These settings will typically be found in the ‘options’ or ‘preferences’ menu of your browser. UnityCoins & Wallet

  1. What is UnityCoins?

    ‘UnityCoins’ is a kind of Wallet Cash and a reward system, which can be used to redeem products on Unity Books Website and App. It is a Non-Transferable Digital Reward.

  2. What is Unity Wallet?

    ‘Unity Wallet’ is a Digital Wallet where your UnityCoins is stored. You can view your Ballance by going to the Unity Wallet.

  3. How to Earn UnityCoins?

    Unity Coins can be Earned in 3 ways:

    • By Selling your old Books on Unity Books, You can earn UnityCoins. The Condition and MRP of the Book would decide the amount of UnityCoins you would earn.
    • From minimum purchase value above ₹150, for every ₹150 you would earn 2 UnityCoins with a maximum of 20 UnityCoins(Order Value for Maximum Rewards is ₹1500).
    • If your Order is returned, you will recieve your refund in UnityCoins. The amount of the UnityCoins received will be same as the Order Value.

  4. When will UnityCoins be Credited in Unity Wallet?

    Once the Return Window Closes, UnityCoins will automatically be credited and could be used for further orders. Once the order is confirmed, you can view your pending UnityCoins which will be credited after the Return Policy period.

  5. What is the Validity of UnityCoins?

    The Validity of UnityCoins is till one year from the date of credit. For e.g: if UnityCoins was credited on 17 April 2022 then, UnityCoins will expire on 17 April 2023.
